Education

I am currently in my Junior year at the University of California, Merced. Being a young and ambitious University, Merced has offered me many opportunities to get involved with faculty and their projects.

I established an online presence for the Society of Physiologists and maintain their webpage using the Drupal CMS at the request of Professor Rudy Ortiz.

Recently I have been working with Professor Angelo Kyrilov to redesign the Compass system. A online submission portal used by upper and lower division CS classes that allows Computer Science and Engineering students to submit their work and receive constructive feedback about their coding logic and style.

This upcoming Spring semester I will be working in the VICE Lab under Professor Joshua Viers Developing geospatial analysis solutions to complex hydrological and agricultural challenges by parsing and analyzing satellite and UAV imagery via Google Earth Engine.

We will be Streamlining processes via Machine Learning, developing multiple Reinforcement Learning models to estimate fauna visitation to stock ponds and utilizing TensorFlow to estimate sediment displacement from landslides in hopes of democratizing our understanding of how natural disasters affect particulate concentrations in watersheds.

I will be graduating in the Spring of 2020.
